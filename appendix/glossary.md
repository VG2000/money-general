### Commercial Bank Money
Deposits in commercial bank accounts. Created when banks make loans. Comprises approximately 97% of UK broad money.

### Broad Money
The total money supply in the economy, including both currency and bank deposits. Measured by aggregates like M4.

## UK Government Institutions

### Consolidated Fund (CF)
The central government account established in 1787. All authorized expenditure originates from the CF, and most revenue flows into it. Functions as a sovereign line of credit backed by Parliament's taxing power.
